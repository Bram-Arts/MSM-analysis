The elements of monsters.
Used when determining possible results from a "normal" breeding attempt where the elements of the parents come into play.
Rares do not need separate entries.
All monsters of which the elements are not important for breeding (e.g. seasonal, mythical) are not entered in this file.
Available elements:
P - Plant
C - Cold
A - Air
W - Water
E - Earth
F - Fire

Li - Light
Ps - Psychic
Fa - Faerie
Bo - Bone

Pl - Plasma
Sh - Shadow
Me - Mech
Cr - Cystal
Po - Poison

Note monsters one on each line like this:
