Monsters that are always available on an island to breed.
These are either declared with a name or with a 1 or 2 letter contraction of their elements.
Limited monsters are in the file availabilities.txt.
Paironormals are in neither, they get a special treatment in the code.
Mirror islands do not need separate entries.

Example:
Plant~P,E,C,W,Shugabush,Ghazt,G'joob

Available elements:
P - Plant
C - Cold
A - Air
W - Water
E - Earth
F - Fire

Li - Light
Ps - Psychic
Fa - Faerie
Bo - Bone

Pl - Plasma
Sh - Shadow
Me - Mech
Cr - Cystal
Po - Poison
